Identify Risks, Reduce Exposure, Stay on Track


Simple steps to protect your plan from surprises


Peterson Financial helps you build a safety net around your goals. We review insurance, cash reserves, and investment risk, then create clear actions—so one event doesn’t derail your future. You’ll know what to keep, what to change, and why.



What We Cover

  1. Income protection: disability coverage levels, elimination periods, and riders
  2. Family protection: term life needs analysis and beneficiary alignment
  3. Long-term care: cost estimates, policy options, and alternatives
  4. Liability & property: umbrella limits, home/auto coordination, deductibles
  5. Cash reserves: emergency fund targets and where to keep the cash
  6. Portfolio risk: allocation, drawdown stress tests, concentration checks, rebalancing
  7. Retirement risks: sequence of returns, inflation, longevity, withdrawal guardrails
  8. Business owners: key-person, buy-sell funding, and entity liability basics
  9. Fraud & cyber: practical steps to secure accounts and reduce identity risk

Your Deliverables

  • Coverage gap report with suggested limits, terms, and next steps
  • Emergency fund target and high-yield savings setup guide
  • Portfolio risk snapshot with stress-test results and rebalancing policy
  • Action checklist to implement changes and review annually

Disclosure: We do not provide legal or tax advice. We can coordinate with licensed agents, your attorney, and CPA to implement recommended changes.
