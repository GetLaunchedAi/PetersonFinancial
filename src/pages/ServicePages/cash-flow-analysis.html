Cash Flow Analysis for Southwest Michigan Rentals & Investments


Underwrite with Confidence—Before You Buy or Refi


Infinity Investment Property Solutions LLC provides professional cash flow analysis for single-family, multi-family, and small commercial assets. We build clean pro formas, verify assumptions, model debt service, and convert raw data into clear recommendations you can act on—buy, hold, improve, or exit.



What We Analyze

  1. Gather documents: rent roll, T-12, utility bills, tax records, insurance, lease terms
  2. Normalize revenue: market rents, loss-to-lease, vacancy/credit loss, other income
  3. Right-size expenses: taxes, insurance, utilities, maintenance, management, reserves
  4. Model financing: rate/term, amortization, IO periods, LTV/LTC, refinance and sale
  5. Stress test: vacancy shocks, rate changes, CapEx schedules, rent growth scenarios
  6. Report: KPIs (NOI, DSCR, CoC, IRR/NPV), break-even, and a clear action plan

What You Get

  • Excel model + PDF summary with assumptions and sensitivity tables
  • 10-year pro forma with cap-ex reserves and debt schedule
  • Valuation snapshots: cap rate, price per door, and return metrics
  • Optional rent comps and renovation ROI recommendations
