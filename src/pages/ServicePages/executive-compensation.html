Stock Options, RSUs & Deferred Compensation


Turn complex compensation into a clear, tax-efficient plan


Peterson Financial helps Executives strategically manage equity awards, bonuses, and deferred comp. We build multi-year tax projections, plan ISO/NSO exercises (including AMT considerations), structure 10b5-1 plans, and create diversification and liquidity strategies that respect blackout periods and insider-trading constraints.



What We Analyze

  1. Grant & vesting: ISOs/NSOs, RSUs/PSUs, ESPP, performance hurdles, and cliffs
  2. Tax modeling: ordinary income vs. capital gains, AMT for ISOs, state mobility impacts
  3. Exercise & sale timing: cashless, sell-to-cover, hold vs. early exercise, 83(b) where applicable
  4. 10b5-1 planning: trading windows, cooling-off, good-faith certification, plan cadence
  5. Deferred comp (409A): deferral elections, distribution timing, and investment menu
  6. Risk & diversification: concentration limits, staged sales, charitable giving (DAF), and hedging considerations

Your Deliverables

  • Equity inventory & vesting calendar with action dates
  • Multi-year tax projection (AMT/NIIT/withholding) and exercise schedule
  • 10b5-1 draft parameters and compliance checklist
  • Diversification roadmap with charitable and estate overlays
