Wills, Trusts & Legacy Planning


Protect your family, minimize taxes, and make your wishes unmistakable


Peterson Financial helps you design an estate plan that fits your life—covering wills and revocable living trusts, durable powers of attorney, healthcare directives, guardianship, TOD/POD designations, trust funding, and strategies to simplify—or avoid—probate. We make the process clear, organized, and action-oriented.



What We Cover

  1. Discovery: family goals, beneficiaries, special considerations, charitable intent
  2. Asset map: account titling, beneficiary designations, TOD/POD, real estate
  3. Document strategy: will vs. revocable trust, POA, healthcare directives
  4. Trust funding plan: deeds, account retitling, beneficiary updates
  5. Tax considerations: step-up in basis, portability, gifting & charitable planning
  6. Implementation & review: coordinate with attorney/CPA and set annual check-ins

Your Deliverables

  • Written estate plan outline with beneficiary & titling checklist
  • Trust funding roadmap and transfer/retitling to-do list
  • Letter of intent for guardians/executors/trustees
  • Review cadence for life events (marriage, children, inheritances, new assets)

Note: We do not provide legal or tax advice. We collaborate with your attorney and CPA to finalize documents and filings.
