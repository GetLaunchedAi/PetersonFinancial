Simple decisions that make a big difference


Peterson Financial helps you map out retirement in plain language. We set savings goals, choose easy-to-manage investments, plan when to start Social Security, estimate healthcare and Medicare costs, and design a tax-smart withdrawal plan so your income feels steady and predictable.



How We Build Your Retirement Plan

  1. Picture your life: travel, hobbies, giving, and must-have expenses
  2. Check your income: Social Security, pensions, rental income, and part-time work
  3. Close the gap: set savings targets and a simple investment mix you can stick with
  4. Pick a start date: choose when to claim Social Security and how to bridge the years before
  5. Plan withdrawals: decide which accounts to use first (Roth, IRA, 401(k), taxable) to help lower taxes
  6. Account for healthcare: estimate Medicare premiums, supplements, and out-of-pocket costs
  7. Adjust as you go: review yearly and make small tweaks as life changes

What You’ll Get

  • Monthly income plan with a simple “which account and how much” guide
  • Social Security timing comparison to help you choose a start age
  • Savings target, automatic contribution setup, and rebalancing schedule
  • Tax-aware withdrawal order and basic Roth conversion guidance (when it helps)
  • Healthcare and Medicare cost estimate in your budget

Notes: Investing involves risk, including loss. We coordinate with your tax and legal pros when needed.
