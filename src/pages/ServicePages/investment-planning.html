Clear Steps to Grow Your Money


Invest with a plan, not a hunch


Peterson Financial builds an investment plan you can stick with. We keep costs low, spread your risk, and place investments in the right accounts for taxes. You’ll know what you own, why you own it, and what to do next.



How We Build Your Portfolio

  1. Understand your goals and timeline (near-term, retirement, college, big purchases)
  2. Set a target mix of stocks, bonds, and cash that fits your comfort with ups and downs
  3. Choose low-cost index funds/ETFs to keep more of your returns
  4. Place assets in the right accounts (Roth/IRA/401(k)/HSA/taxable) to reduce taxes
  5. Automate contributions and set a simple rebalancing schedule
  6. Review regularly and adjust as life changes

What’s Included

  • Target allocation and fund list you can follow
  • Account setup and transfer checklist
  • Monthly saving targets and auto-invest instructions
  • Guidance on withdrawals and “which account first” when you need money
  • Tax-aware moves like asset location and, when appropriate, tax-loss harvesting

Notes: Investing involves risk, including loss. Diversification can’t guarantee profits or prevent losses. We coordinate with your tax and legal pros when needed.
