About Peterson Financial

With more than 20 years of experience in the financial services industry, Scott is a trusted Registered Investment Advisor (RIA) who’s passionate about helping individuals, families, and business owners plan for their future with confidence and clarity. As the founder of MMX Wealth Partners, he brings together deep industry expertise and a personal, down-to-earth approach to deliver thoughtful, customized wealth management strategies.

Scott focuses on areas like retirement planning, investment strategy, tax-efficient portfolios, and estate planning—always offering clear, objective advice tailored to each client’s unique goals. His clients value his honesty, transparency, and strong commitment to always putting their best interests first—no matter the market conditions.

Before launching MMX Wealth Partners, Scott worked as a Financial Advisor at Ameriprise Financial and Ballast Advisors, where he built a strong foundation navigating complex financial situations and evolving regulations. He continues to stay current with industry trends and best practices through ongoing education and professional development. Outside of work, Scott enjoys hitting the golf course, traveling, and cheering on his kids at their Big Ten schools—Michigan State and Penn State—especially during football and basketball season.
